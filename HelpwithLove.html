HOW OUR PROJECT WORK

Help With Love is a charitable organization dedicated to supporting those in need by promoting tangible assistance and solidarity. We work everyday to make a difference in the lives of those who need a helping hand. Our mission is to provide help to the vulnerable ones through volunteering, frunsdraising and support programs.

The steps of our job:

  1. Collect: thank to ours stands and events we can collect goods usefull for the ones that need them. If you want to help you can check out the list of essentials goods.

  2. Organize: after collect the products we always check them and organize in orther to best distribute products as needed

  3. Distribute: following the requests we have collected we organize, thanks to all the volonteers, distribution days

Attend upcoming events


First, from week to week you can find our food and non-food collection stands outside the major supermarkets in the area. While on a monthly basis we hold larger events directly at our centre location. There you can also participate in the behind-the-scenes organization and distribution.

Be a part of the team!


Being a volunteer means joining a network of people united by a shared mission to improve lives. Through their dedication, vital resources like food, clothing, and basic necessities are delivered to those in need. Every action, from gathering to distributing, creates a tangible impact, turning generosity into hope.


Being a volunteer member means taking on the responsibility of actively contributing to the well-being of the community by dedicating time and skills to a common goal, with commitment, dedication, and respect for others.
